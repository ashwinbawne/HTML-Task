Tata Consultancy Services

Tata Consultancy Services is an Indian multinational information technology services and consulting company with its headquarters in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
It is a part of the Tata Group and operates in 150 locations across 46 countries.
In July 2022, it was reported that TCS had over 600,000 employees worldwide.
